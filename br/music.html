My relationship with music

My music interests range from progressive rock and jazz to classical and even electronic music.

Some years ago I used to be a member of a progressive rock band, from which I split since I moved to France. We released the album The Awakening Barrier in which I play the keyboards and that contains some compositions of mine. If you have Spotify, you can listen to it here.

Many years ago I used to produce electronic music, which was my first experience in composing music. If you want to take a look at what I done, you can listen to it here. Looking back at those tracks, many of them (specially the first ones) are very bad, but there are a few that I still think that are good ones and that I sometimes enjoy listening to.

Nowadays I'm mainly working on my piano skills and learning to play classical music. I'm also taking a course on counterpoint writing.
